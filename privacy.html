Short version: we collect your email when you sign up, use it to send you the download and occasional updates, and never sell it. That's it.

1. What we collect

Data When
Email address When you submit the signup form
IP address Automatically, on each page visit (via Vercel hosting)
Basic analytics Page views and referrer data via Vercel Analytics
Payment details If you purchase a product — handled entirely by Stripe, not us

We do not collect names, phone numbers, or any other personal information unless you voluntarily provide it when contacting us.

2. Why we collect it

Your email address is collected for three purposes: to deliver your download, to send you product updates when we release new guides or skill files (at most once a week), and to respond if you reach out to us.

IP addresses and analytics data are used only to understand general traffic patterns and improve the site. We do not use them to identify individual users.

3. Where it's stored

Email addresses are stored in Resend, an email delivery service. Payment data is handled by Stripe — we never see or store your card details.

No other third-party data processors are used. Both Resend and Stripe are GDPR-compliant services.

4. Third parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Ever.

The only services that ever touch your data are Resend (email delivery), Stripe (payments), and Vercel (hosting and analytics) — and only to the extent needed to operate the site.

5. Cookies and analytics

We do not set any cookies on this site.

Vercel Analytics is used for basic, privacy-friendly page view tracking. It does not use cookies and does not track users across sites. The data collected is aggregate and not linked to individual identities.

6. How long we keep it

Your email address is kept until you unsubscribe or request deletion. Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link.

If you delete your account or request erasure, we will remove your email from Resend within 30 days.

Analytics data is retained per Vercel's standard retention policy and is not linked to personal identifiers.

7. Your rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU or UK, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access — You can ask what data we hold about you.

Rectification — You can ask us to correct inaccurate data.

Erasure — You can ask us to delete your data.

Portability — You can ask for a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.

Objection — You can object to how we use your data at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us on X (see below). We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority — in Sweden, that is the Integritetsskyddsmyndigheten (IMY).
